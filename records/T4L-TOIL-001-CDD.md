## 2. Problem Statement

HVAC condensate drain line blockages are a frequent and costly failure point that can result in:

- Water overflow and property damage
- Mold growth and associated health risks
- Equipment shutdowns and service interruptions

Existing solutions typically respond after overflow has already occurred and do not enable proactive inspection or structured preventative maintenance.

---

## 3. Solution Overview

The Clean Drain Device is a compact, inline HVAC condensate drain adapter designed to:

- Enable early clog detection  
- Restrict backflow  
- Allow direct flushing and maintenance  
- Provide visual inspection of internal flow

The device integrates mechanical flow control, optional sensor-trigger activation, and a maintenance access port into a unified structural adapter.

---

## 4. Core Functional Elements

- Transparent inspection body  
- Directional internal valve to restrict reverse flow  
- Optional sensor-trigger mechanism (reed switch, Hall sensor, or float-based variants)  
- Electrical signal output capability for HVAC shutdown integration  
- Threaded flush/maintenance port  
- Optional additional compatibility ports

The device is designed to function in both vertical and horizontal installations.
